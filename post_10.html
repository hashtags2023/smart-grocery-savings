Amazon Fresh Stores Are Closing in 2026 — What It Means for Grocery Shoppers

🚨 Breaking News — January 2026

Amazon announced on January 27, 2026 that it is permanently closing all 72 Amazon Fresh and Amazon Go physical stores nationwide.

If you've been shopping at an Amazon Fresh or Amazon Go store, you may have shown up recently to find the doors locked and a closure notice on the window. Amazon made a surprise announcement on January 27, 2026 that it's shutting down all of its branded brick-and-mortar grocery stores — all 57 Amazon Fresh locations and 15 Amazon Go convenience stores across the United States. Here's everything you need to know about what closed, what still works, and where to shop instead.

57 Amazon Fresh stores closed
15 Amazon Go stores closed
100+ New Whole Foods stores planned

What Happened and Why

Amazon had been experimenting with physical grocery stores for nearly a decade, most notably with its "Just Walk Out" cashierless technology in Amazon Go stores and its full-size Amazon Fresh supermarkets. Despite significant investment, the stores never found a compelling enough reason to exist — they were, as one retail analyst put it, too similar to competitors without offering anything truly distinctive.

In an official statement, Amazon said it wasn't able to create a "truly distinctive customer experience with the right economic model needed for large-scale expansion." Meanwhile, its online grocery delivery business was booming — perishable grocery sales through its Same-Day Delivery service grew significantly in 2025, and fresh items became the most ordered products in areas where same-day delivery was available.

The decision was part of a broader strategic reset at Amazon, which is also expanding Whole Foods Market — a brand it acquired in 2017 — to more than 100 new locations over the next several years.

Timeline of Closures

  • 1

    January 27, 2026 — Announcement

    Amazon announced the closures without warning. Many stores locked their doors the same day, leaving shoppers and employees by surprise.

  • 2

    February 1, 2026 — Most stores close

    The majority of Amazon Fresh and Amazon Go locations across the US closed permanently on February 1, 2026.

  • 3

    March 13, 2026 — California stores close

    California's 22 Amazon Fresh locations stayed open an additional 45 days due to state labor notification requirements before closing on March 13, 2026.

  • 4

    Coming soon — Some locations convert to Whole Foods

    Amazon is evaluating which former Fresh locations to convert into Whole Foods Market stores, though specific locations and timelines haven't been announced yet.

What Amazon Fresh Still Offers

Here's the important thing — Amazon Fresh online delivery is still fully operational. The closures only affect physical stores. If you have Amazon Prime, you can still order groceries online at amazon.com/fresh and get same-day or next-day delivery in most areas.

💡 Still works with Amazon Prime: Online grocery delivery through amazon.com/fresh is still active in the vast majority of areas. Prime members get free delivery on orders over $35, and same-day delivery on fresh produce and perishables is now available in over 2,300 cities.

The Bottom Line

The closure of Amazon Fresh physical stores is a big shift but it doesn't mean Amazon is leaving the grocery space — far from it. The company is doubling down on online delivery and Whole Foods expansion instead.

For most shoppers, the practical impact is minimal. If you used Amazon Fresh for delivery, that service still works online through Prime. If you used the physical stores, Walmart+, Instacart, and Whole Foods are the best replacements depending on your priorities.

The bigger story here is that grocery delivery is winning over physical stores — and that's actually good news for budget-conscious shoppers who want convenience without paying full retail price.
