Affiliate Disclosure

We believe you deserve to know exactly how this site works and how we make money.

Last updated: April 2025

The short version: Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. We only recommend services and products we've genuinely evaluated. Commissions never influence our rankings or opinions.

🔗 What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking URLs. When you click one and complete a purchase or sign up for a service, the retailer pays us a small referral fee. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you had gone directly to their website.

This is a common way for content websites to earn revenue, and it allows us to keep Smart Grocery Savings free for everyone.

🧭 Our Editorial Promise

Our content is driven by research and honest evaluation — not by which companies pay the highest commissions. Here's what that means in practice:

🔍

Independent Research

We compare prices and features ourselves. No company pays to be featured or ranked higher.

📊

Real Data

Our comparisons use actual prices and terms at the time of writing, not marketing materials.

🚫

No Paid Reviews

We do not accept payment for positive reviews or sponsored placements disguised as editorial content.
