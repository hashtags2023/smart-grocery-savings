Quick Savings Tips
  • 📋

    Always shop with a list

    Shoppers without a list spend an average of 23% more per trip due to impulse purchases.

  • 🏷️

    Compare unit prices, not shelf prices

    The bigger package isn't always cheaper. Check the price per ounce on the shelf tag.

  • 📱

    Stack cashback apps with store sales

    Apps like Ibotta and Fetch Rewards work on top of existing sales — double the savings on the same item.

  • 🥦

    Buy produce that's in season

    In-season produce can cost 50–70% less than out-of-season equivalents.
