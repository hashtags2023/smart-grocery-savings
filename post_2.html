Walmart+ vs Instacart: Which Saves You More?

If you’re looking to save on groceries, both Walmart+ and Instacart are popular options. Let’s compare them:

Walmart+

  • Fast delivery from Walmart stores
  • Free shipping on orders over $35
  • Subscription cost: $12.95/month

Sign up for Walmart+

Instacart

  • Shop from multiple stores in your area
  • Delivery fees vary, free delivery on orders over $35 with subscription
  • Subscription: Instacart Express $9.99/month

Try Instacart here

Which Should You Choose?

Both services save time and energy. If you mostly shop at Walmart, Walmart+ might be cheaper. If you like multiple stores or specialty items, Instacart is better.
